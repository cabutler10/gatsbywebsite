Data Scientist, Front End Web Developer

I am a Data Scientist with a strong interest in Web Development. I am fascinated with the idea of being able to make data driven decision making something everyone can use and understand by building online and mobile tools, charts, and tutorials.

I am a strong believer that data analyses shouldn't be hard to do or to understand. What good is data analysis if it sits buried in lengthy articles that don't get read? What I love about Web Development is that is provides an opportunity for data analyses to be something everyone can use.
